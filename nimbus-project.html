Nimbus Conference

Event branding - Promotional Materials - Environmental

Branding for the Nimbus event: an international conference on flooding and preparation for emergency crises hosted by the British Red Cross in Belfast. The logo is typeset with a yellow beacon underlining 'us' highlighting the importance of working together in times of crisis. The strong warning red is toned down together with complementary coloured abstract shapes representing rain drops and water. Project created whilst at Whitenoise Studios.
