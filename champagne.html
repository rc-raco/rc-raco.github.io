Champagne: A Wayfarer's Story

Packaging - Branding - Storytelling - Lasercutting

This 18th century story by Anton Chekhov, is retold through the medium of luxury packaging. It begins with a luxurious weighted gift box - designed to entice with hints of rich gold foil and a tear open tab along the front. It is filled with layers, each one revealing more of the story. However, once opened, it can never returned to its original state as delicate materials like tissue crumple and tear upon opening. It is destructive packaging inspired by the main character’s selfish actions which change the course of his family’s life forever.
