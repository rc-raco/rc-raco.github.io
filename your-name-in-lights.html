Your Name in Lights

Layout & Editorial - Hand Lettering - Illustration

A showcase printed piece for Belfast’s Grand Opera House theatre directed at the corporate sponsorship market. An informative piece filled with fun, illustrative infographics highlighting the achievements of the well known establishment. The cover was finished with a soft touch paper and the hand lettered cover design was brought to life with gold foiling. Project created whilst at Whitenoise Studios.
