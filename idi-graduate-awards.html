IDI Graduate Awards

Event Branding - Exhibition display

A collaborative project for the 2014 IDI: Institute Designers Ireland Graduate Awards. The brief was to design for the annual event including the event branding, exhibition graphics, promotional posters and flyers and the awards themselves. The concept was inspired by graduates plotting their future careers after graduation. This was translated into a visual language of maps represented by topographic lines, cross hairs, scales and location coordinates. The geographic coordinates of each nominated graduate's college featured across the map.
